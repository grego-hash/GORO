INTRODUCTION
Hager Companies offers a variety of locks to meet a wide range of applications and solutions. Heavy Duty Grade 1 deadlocks, 
cylindrical locks, and mortise locks are ideal for schools, hospitals, and other high-use environments. Grade 2 deadlocks and cylindrical 
locks are ideal for less abusive environments, while Grade 2 tubular locks are suitable for heavy duty residential or medium duty 
commercial environments.

All Hager locks have been rigorously tested and certified to the highest standards. Most lock series are BHMA certified per the ANSI 
A156.2 standards. They are also UL/cUL Listed for all functions up to and including 3 hours for “A” labeled doors. In addition to the 
certifications, all Hager Grade 1 and Grade 2 leversets are ADA compliant and most come with a lifetime warranty.

Most Hager locksets are either non-handed or field reversible in the case of the 3800 Series mortise lock. Conventional cylinder and 
small format interchangeable core are available on keyed functions. Most lock series offer three lever style options along with one knob 
style. Please check appropriate section for series specific options.
